📱 How to Export Your Chat

Follow these steps to export a WhatsApp chat on your device.

🤖 Android

  1. Open the WhatsApp group chat
  2. Tap the three dots (⋮) → More → Export chat
  3. Select Without Media
  4. Share/save the .txt file to your device

🍎 iPhone

  1. Open the WhatsApp group chat
  2. Tap the group name at the top
  3. Scroll down → Export Chat
  4. Select Without Media

What You Get

📊

Exploratory Data Analysis

Interactive charts showing message frequency over time, most active members, peak hours and days, media sharing patterns, and monthly trends. Helps identify group behavior and communication patterns at a glance.

🧠

Sentiment Analysis

Powered by VADER NLP — classifies every message as positive, negative, or neutral. Shows per-member emotional breakdown helping analysts identify tension, conflicts, or unusual behavioral shifts in the group.

🔍

Forensic Analysis

Scans messages for 50+ criminal keywords, extracts all shared links with sender attribution, and builds a knowledge graph showing communication flows between members. Essential for law enforcement and investigation use cases.

☁️

Word Cloud & Vocabulary

Visual word cloud of the most frequently used terms, filtered for stopwords in both English and Hindi. Reveals the core topics and interests of the group.

😂

Emoji Intelligence

Tracks the most used emojis in the group. Emoji usage patterns reveal the emotional tone and cultural dynamics — a formal group uses fewer emojis than a casual friend group.

🗓️

Activity Heatmap

Month × day-of-week heatmap pinpoints exactly when the group is most communicative. Unusual spikes can indicate events, conflicts, or coordinated activity — valuable for forensic timeline reconstruction.
